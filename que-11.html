Add some space (margin) before and after the element.   Inline elements cannot contain block-level elements. 
 Examples of block-level elements include `<p>`, `<div>`, `<h1>` to `<h6>`, `<ul>`, `<ol>`, and `<table>`.  Examples of inline elements include `<span>`, `<strong>`, `<em>`, `<a>`, and `<img>`. 
Block-level elements are often used for creating sections, paragraphs, lists, and containers for other elements.  Inline elements are commonly used for styling specific parts of text or adding links.
