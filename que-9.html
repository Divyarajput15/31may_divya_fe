how is cell padding is different from cell spacing ? with example

Cell padding and cell spacing are two attributes used in table formatting in HTML. The main difference between them lies in their purpose and effect. Cell padding controls the space between the border of a table cell and its contents. It specifies the whitespace between the cell edge and the content of the cell. This attribute is defined using the `cellpadding` property within the `
