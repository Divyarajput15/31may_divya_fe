what are the tags and attributes in html ?

HTML tags are used in HTML to define the structure and appearance of elements on a webpage. Tags are enclosed in angle brackets ("<" and ">") and come in pairs, consisting of an opening tag and a closing tag. The opening tag indicates the beginning of an element, while the closing tag indicates the end of the element. For example, the `

They are used within the opening tag of an element and are written as name-value pairs. Attributes modify the behavior or appearance of an element. For instance, the `href` attribute in the `` tag specifies the hyperlink URL, and the `src` attribute in the `` tag specifies the image source. It's important to note that HTML tags define the elements, while attributes provide additional information or instructions for those elements.
